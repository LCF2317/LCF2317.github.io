Frequent asked question


1. What types of coolers do you offer?
We offer a variety of coolers, including hard-sided coolers, soft-sided coolers, backpack coolers, and more. Each type serves different purposes and comes in various sizes.
2. What sizes are available for your coolers?
Our coolers range in size from small, compact options suitable for personal use to larger coolers suitable for groups or extended trips.
3. Are your coolers insulated?
Yes, all our coolers are insulated to maintain the temperature of your items for an extended period.
4. What materials are used in the construction of your coolers?
Our coolers are typically made of high-quality materials such as durable plastic, metal, and insulated fabrics to ensure sturdiness and insulation.
5. Can I use your coolers for both hot and cold items?
Yes, our coolers are suitable for both hot and cold items. They effectively keep items at the desired temperature.
6. Do your coolers come with any warranties?
Yes, we offer warranties on our coolers. The warranty period and coverage details may vary depending on the specific product. Please refer to the product description for warranty information.
7. How long does the ice last in your coolers?
The ice retention time in our coolers can vary based on the cooler's size, insulation, and environmental conditions. Generally, our coolers can keep ice for several days.
8. Are your coolers leak-proof?
Yes, our coolers are designed to be leak-proof to prevent any spillage or leakage of liquids.
9. Can I customize or personalize a cooler with my own design or logo?
Some of our coolers offer customization options. Please check our product customization section or contact our customer service for more information on personalization options.
10. What is your return or exchange policy for coolers?
We have a 30-day return policy for unused coolers. Please refer to our return/exchange policy page for detailed information on our procedures and conditions.
